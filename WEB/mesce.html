MES COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Approved by AICTE, Affiliated to APJ Abdhul Kalam Tecnological University

Biggest educational movement of the muslim community in INDIA'S history

MES College of Engineering is a Kuttippuram, Malappuram-based private engineering college. It is Kerala's first
self-financing engineering college, with a campus that extends for more than a mile alongside the Bharathappuzha River.
It was founded in 1994 as a minority-serving institution
